FOAMING IN HER EYES is a performance series by drag specialist in computer ophthalmology ANNE AMPERSAND. Each 30 minute episode consists of choreographed layers of sequences featuring amorphous talking heads, blurry alien landscapes and estranged objects produced by feeding curated Image and video material from both found and personal archives into machine learning algorithms. Instead of analytically pointing to the issue of data bias, Anne Ampersand searches for moments in which the unincluded still manages to make an appearance. Like an air bubble stuck between signal and noise, its presence is felt as a movement trapped in digital matter. A foam, popping and rippling in different rhythms.
