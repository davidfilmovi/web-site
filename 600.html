This fragmentary episode follows Anne Ampersand’s employment by an unknown chain of clubs and restaurants on the Adriatic coast, requesting unwanted items to be removed from a large number of stock photos acquired for promotional purposes. One of the unwanted ‘objects’ present in the image files is the Mediterranean Monk Seal. Ampersand notes that the number of photographs of the Mediterranean Monk Seal (less than 600) coincides with the number of surviving individuals of this highly endangered species of seal. Recognizing this coincidence as a type of magical entanglement, Ampersand seeks a way to preserve the cutout figures.
